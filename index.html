Iron Patriots

FRC team 4131 from Liberty High School

Welcome to the Iron Patriots' website!

The Liberty High School Robotics Club consists of students ranging from freshman to senior year, some of whom attend other schools than Liberty or participate in the Running Start program.

We are almost into our sixth build season in the FIRST FRC Competition.

If you are interested in joining, please make contact with one of the officers or simply show up at a meeting. Our meeting times along with additional information will be posted on our calendar.
